Odenburgh is a Medieval-style calligraphy typeface. 
Hand-drawn by Måns Grebäck during 2018-2020, this high-quality lettering is inspired by historical Gaelic, British and Irish handwriting. 
It comes as a regular, clean style as well as the additional Odenburgh Deco style. 

The typeface is perfect for calligraphic headlines, products and logotypes in Middle Ages projects. 

The font supports all European, Latin-based languages.
In addition to that, it contains numbers and all punctuation and symbols you'll ever need.
